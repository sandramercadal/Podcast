What is a career break?


Many professionals decide to take an intentional break from their careers at some point. Whether by choice or circumstance, it often comes with a complex mix of emotions and implications. The reasons for stepping away from work vary widely, including caregiving responsibilities, health issues, relocation, redundancy, or pursuing personal and professional development. A career break can be a period rich with growth and learning. Embracing the insights gained during this time can be a key to unlocking the next stage of your career.
