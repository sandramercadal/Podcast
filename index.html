What is a career break?

Many professionals decide to take an intentional break from their careers at some point. Whether by choice or circumstance, it often comes with a complex mix of emotions and implications.
The reasons for stepping away from work vary widely, including caregiving responsibilities, health issues, relocation, redundancy, or pursuing personal and professional development to name a few.
